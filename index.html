The world today

Four clinics. One spreadsheet. Something has to give.

You've grown from one practice to four — and the systems that worked when it was a single clinic are becoming a quiet bottleneck. Here's what that looks like day to day:

📋

Referrer data lives in Excel

Every GP, specialist, and dental practitioner is tracked in spreadsheets. Updates happen when someone remembers to make them. There's no single source of truth across your four clinics.

🔁

Comrade RIS exports are manual

Pulling referral activity out of Comrade and into a usable view takes hours every week. The data is always slightly out of date by the time someone looks at it.

👀

No visibility across clinics

A practice manager at one site has no easy way to see what their counterpart at another site is doing. Visits, follow-ups, and wins go untracked or live in someone's inbox.

📉

You can't see who's drifting away

A reliable GP referrer drops off — and you don't notice for three months. By then, the relationship has cooled and someone else has filled the gap.

⚡
Every one of these problems is fixable today. The next four clinics' worth of growth depends on not solving them with more spreadsheets.
